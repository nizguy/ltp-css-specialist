The Raven

Edgar Allen Poe

Once upon a midnight dreary, While I pondered, weak and weary, Over many a quaint and curious volume of forgottenlore-- While I nodded, nearly napping, suddenly there came a tapping, as of someone gently rapping, rapping at my chamber door. " 'Tis some visitor," I muttered, "tapping at my chamber door-- Only this and nothing more."
